Introducing MediaPlay from Cell C.

It’s a revolutionary new way to connect not only to your friends, family and colleagues, but to your favourite movies, TV shows, social media and websites too. MediaPlay bundles a regular cellular contract with absolutely everything you need to access everything you want: a subscription to black (our entertainment platform), unlimited data exclusively used for watching black, and all the free minutes, data and texts you need to use your phone… well, as a phone!

Simply put, MediaPlay is your all-access entertainment and communication package that empowers you to connect to whatever you want to connect to.

What you get

A world of entertainment on black

black is an entertainment offering like no other in South Africa! Get access to movies, local and international series, music, kids’ programming, documentaries, news, sport and more, all on one platform.

Exclusive data for black

MediaPlay doesn’t just offer you tons of great content, we give you the data to enjoy it too! You can binge to your heart’s content with unlimited data to watch whatever you want on black without incurring any data costs.

Voice and data

In addition to all the cool stuff you can enjoy on black, and your choice of how you connect to it, MediaPlay also offers you all the benefits and freebies that come with a mobile contract.
