Introducing MediaPlay from Cell C.

It’s a revolutionary new way to connect not only to your friends, family and colleagues, but to your favourite movies, TV shows, social media and websites too. MediaPlay bundles a regular cellular contract with absolutely everything you need to access everything you want: a subscription to black (our entertainment platform), unlimited data exclusively used for watching black, and all the free minutes, data and texts you need to use your phone… well, as a phone!

Simply put, MediaPlay is your all-access entertainment and communication package that empowers you to connect to whatever you want to connect to.
